Benedict Steele works with organisations where technology delivery is stuck or slow, and helps them see what's actually causing it. That might be the teams, how the organisation is set up, or how decisions get made, or something else entirely. He then works inside the organisation to change it - through the work itself, not from a slide deck.

Benedict has led transformation as an executive and a consultant across startups, large charities, and regulated public-sector organisations. He co-founded and grew a digital consultancy, led a technology team delivering a product portfolio at Cancer Research UK, and has spent the last seven years as a senior leader consulting for government departments, financial services, and healthcare organisations. He now works directly with leadership teams, helping them redesign how their organisations deliver.
